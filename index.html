Originally trained as an architect, I moved to Los Angeles in 2018 to work alongside Frank Gehry in his design studio.

While starting at a firm that uses modelmaking as its primary design tool, I learned a lot in physical craft and gained an enormous appreciation for Frank's process of form-finding rooted in his innate curiosity for testing new materials and pushing their tectonic limitations.

In 2023, I co-founded a creative studio called Postmodern Tectonics.

We design spaces, we build furniture, strategize brands, help on marketing, and then we like to publish essays along the way.
