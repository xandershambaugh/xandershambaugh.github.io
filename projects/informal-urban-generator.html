An Informal Urban Generator: Redefining the Human-Trash Relationship

Advisor: Robin Dripps / University of Virginia / 2017 — 2018
New Delhi, India
Figure ground map

Overview

A comprehensive thesis investigating the informal waste management systems of New Delhi, India. This project explores the relationship between urban waste infrastructure and informal settlements, proposing new frameworks for integrated waste management that leverage existing informal networks.
